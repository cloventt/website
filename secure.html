S/MIME

I support S/MIME for message signing and encryption on my email address.

To use it, email me with your S/MIME signature enabled and let me know you prefer to use encryption.

In some cases I may need you to send me your S/MIME public certificate in a .pem file seperately (for instance, if your certificate is lost in transit to my email server).

If you want to encrypt communications immediately, my S/MIME public certificate is available here also.
